Overview

Purpose

Our purpose to help american households become self sufficient and save money by going green and generating electricity with solar systems. Solar panels can be expensive, so we offer a solar panel leasing program to qualifying home owners!

Audience

Our target audience are middle to high class households that are looking to save money on their electrical bill or want to go green.

Home page

[Written copy for the home page here]

1. Educate: At Solar Island, we believe that information leads to inspiration. Our website serves for educating on all questions solar, sourcing solar energy with panel installation, and more. By visiting our site, you can expand your understanding of solar power and make informed decisions about adopting this eco-friendly technology. 2. Solar Products: We offer solar panels from trusted manufacturers, ensuring quality, durability, and maximum energy efficiency. By visiting our website, you can browse through our extensive catalog, you can learn more about the panel that will be installed on your roof and which battery options would be best to store your excess energy. 3.Expert Advice: Solar Island is committed to assisting you throughout your solar journey. From answering your questions to system design and installation, we tailor your solar experience to your needs. Solar Island allows you to submit inquiries, and schedule consultations with our team. We are here to answer your questions and provide the knowledge you need to transition into your self-sustaining future. 4. Financinces: Going solar is a big investment. We provide a multitude of financing options, and present government incentives. We will provide the tools to help you explore the financial aspects of going solar, so you can join the solar family.
