Halo: An Epic

Through rings, a hero rises, Master Chief, battles surprises.

Alien hordes, his might withstands,

Halo Combat Evolved, epic lands.

With Cortana's aid, they persist, Facing Flood's horror

their will persists. Secrets unveiled, fate unfurled,

A universe saved, their legend unfurled.

Warthogs and Banshees, battles unfold, Halo's embrace,

our hearts behold. A masterpiece divine,

memories enshrined, Halo Combat Evolved, forever entwined.

Through the annals of gaming, its presence endures, Halo,

a saga that ensures, In the hearts of gamers,

its memory held dear, A timeless adventure, forever revered.
